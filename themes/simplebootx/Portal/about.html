Company OverView

JINGWEI Textile Machinery Co.,Ltd.(herein after referred to as “JINGWEI”),a subordinate of CHINA HI-TECH GROUP CORPORATION. JINGWEI takes textile machinery as principal business and financial & Trust business as well. JINGWEI is a public listed company on SHENZHEN Stock Exchange, Stock code: 000666.

As the global leading manufacturer of complete set of cotton spinning chain equipment, JINGWEI inherits the essence of the national textile machinery, provides a solid equipment guarantee for the development of China cotton spinning industry. Focused in textile machinery and market segments for more than 60 years, JINGWEI gradually form”six product units” pattern, i.e. cotton spinning, weaving, textile machinery textile accessories,twisting, knitting, and printing & dyeing. With the development of textile industry and customer's requirements, JINGWEI keeps to invest in R&D to innovate service model helping customers build "smart textile factory",which acts as an impetus for company to provide complete sets of textile machinery equipment and full-process quality services to help JINGWEI earn a good image in the market.

JINGWEI actively promoted industrial and financial integration, invested in financial trust business, accelerated the development of the textile industry, and achieved good results.. As a public listed company that landed in the capital market earlier in China, JINGWEI optimized the corporate governance to construct a harmonious relationship among shareholders, society, environment, employees and other stakeholders.

By December 31, 2016, JINGWEI had total assets of 35.3 billion yuan, operating revenue of 1.04 billion yuan, total profit of 3 billion yuan, and asset-liability ratio of 55.08 %.

1951, JINGWEI Textile Machinery set up factories in Yuci, Shanxi province, was the largest and newest textile factory in China that time.
31st, May, 1959, the former Vice Premier and Foreign Minister of the State Council, Marshal Chen Yi, visited the factory, wrote the company name for JINGWEI.
1985, Ring frame FA502 honored National Sliver Medal for machinery quality, this is the best prize since JINGWEI set up.
1991,the State Council Enterprise Steering Committee ratified JINGWEI as the national first level enterprise
1995, JINGWEI Textile Machinery Co., Ltd set up and be approved as National High and New Tech Enterprise
1996, JINGWEI was approved as “Foreign Capital holding Corporation”, landed on Hongkong and Shenzhen stock exchanges, become a state owned holding company with international capitals ground .
1999, Under China Textile Machinery Group, Ltd (“CTMG”)strategic business plan, CTMG acquired four subsidiaries to help JINGWEI become the first enterprise that can supply complete equipment sets of cotton spinning.
2000,JINGWEI headquartered in Beijing, issued 18 thousands A shares, set up a technology edged R&D centre.
2002, JINGWEI acquired 95% shareholdings of CHANGDE TEXTILE MACHINERY CO., LTD, and 64.38% of YICHANGTEXTILE MACHINERY CO., LTD, Invested in setting up WUXI JINGWEI TEXTILE TECHNOLOGY SET CO., LTD. In the same year, Beijing “JINGWEI Technology Industrial Zone” put into operation.
2003，JINGWEI acquired 28% shareholdings of Shanxi Jingwei Heli Machinery Co.,Ltd .
2005, JINGWEI acquired 40% WUXI TEXPARTS MACHINERY CO., LTD, and become executive council member of China Cotton Textile Association.
2008, JINGWEI launched Roving-Ring Link Conveying and “Jingwei e-system” in ITMA, Shanghai, which was a symbol of China made textile machinery automation and intelligence.
2009, JINGWEI was one of the first batch textile enterprises to public Social Responsibility report .
2010, JINGWEI recapitalized HUBEI XINCHUFENG AUTOMOBILE CO., LTD, and acquired 36% shareholdings of ZHONGRONG INTERNATIONAL TRUST CO.,LTD, that brought JINGWEI step into non- textile industry.
2011, JINGWEI business income more than 10 billion yuan, total profit more than 1.5billon yuan .
2012, JINGWEI issued unlisted 100330 thousand A shares;interim notes issued 1.2billon yuan.
2013, JINGWEI acquired 51% shareholdings of Slovenia DURABUS Co., Ltd, and Netherlands GINAF heavy trucks project. These two acquisition marked JINGWEI commercial automobile business internationalize.
2014, established cotton textile machinery business unit which promote JINGWEI from “complete equipment set of single machinery” to “complete equipment set of industrial projects supplier”; at the same time, JINGWEI expanded Trust business register capitals, and set up HI-TECH New Energy Automobile Co., Ltd.
2015, JINGWEI delisted from Hongkong stock exchange; “Textile digital workshop” project got through national acceptance which was a impetus to push JINGWEI product upgrading.
